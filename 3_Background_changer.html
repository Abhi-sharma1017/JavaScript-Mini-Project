About us

The Taj Mahal, an architectural marvel in Agra, India, stands as a testament to love and craftsmanship. Commissioned by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal, its construction began in 1632. This iconic monument has captivated hearts worldwide with its timeless beauty and historical significance.
The Taj Mahal is a beautiful white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Constructed in the 17th century, it is renowned for its intricate architecture and exquisite craftsmanship. The main structure stands elegantly on a raised platform, surrounded by lush gardens and reflecting pools. The intricate designs on the marble surfaces depict intricate floral motifs and calligraphy from the Quran. The Taj Mahal is considered a symbol of eternal love and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, attracting millions of visitors annually.
